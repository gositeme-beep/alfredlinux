Title
Full of Mercy

Style of Music
Hebrew jewish song gosoel in hebrew for god


אדון כל הארץ מלא רחמים
Adon kol ha’aretz maleh rachamim
→ Lord of all the earth, full of mercy

ונשגב שמך על כל גויים
V’nishgav shimkha al kol goyim
→ Exalted is Your Name above all nations

ומלכותך עומדת לעד
Umalchutkha omedet la’ad
→ And Your kingdom stands forever

בכל דור ודור
B’chol dor vador
→ In every generation
