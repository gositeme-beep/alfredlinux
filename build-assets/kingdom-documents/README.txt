Kingdom documents — sovereign chronicle on disk (honest bytes toward ~7.77 GiB)
================================================================================

This folder is copied into the live ISO by hook 0285 §7 together with videos and
wallpapers → /usr/share/alfred-linux/kingdom-media/documents/

What belongs here (examples)
-----------------------------
• PDF or HTML exports of Official Acts from The People's Journal (e.g. Act N° 006 —
  Public Release of the Authorized King Jesus Version — Perez Family Edition), with
  canonical web reference: https://lavocat.ca/journal?read=9&lang=en
• Short timeline: April 8, 2026 A.D. (Act N° 002 and related milestones) through
  April 10, 2026 A.D. (public AKJV release decree) — your words, your seals.
• Perez family story: curated photos (prefer also ship high-res under
  build-assets/wallpapers/4k or 8k), captions, and optional short narrated video
  under build-assets/videos/ (that is where the largest honest weight lives).

Licensing
---------
Only place material you have the right to redistribute inside the ISO. For huge
reference corpora (e.g. Wikipedia / Kiwix .zim), prefer a second torrent or post-install
bundle per docs/ISO-777-GiB-PLAN.txt — do not bloat the hybrid ISO past what you can
boot-test unless you widen the size gate on purpose.
