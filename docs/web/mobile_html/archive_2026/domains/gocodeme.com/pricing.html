Questions & Answers

What are AI tokens?
Tokens are the units AI models use to process text. Roughly 1 token ≈ 4 characters. A 100-line code file is ~2,000 tokens. We only count output tokens against your plan — reading your code is free.
Which AI models are included?
All plans include Claude Haiku 4.5 and 5 open-source models (Qwen3 Coder, DeepSeek V3, GLM-5, Kimi K2.5, Llama 4 Maverick). Paid plans add Claude Sonnet 4.6. Studio+ adds Claude Opus 4.6. Our smart router automatically picks the best model for each task to save your tokens.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades apply at your next billing cycle.
What happens when I run out of tokens?
You can buy token packs for one-time extra tokens, or upgrade your plan. Your work is never lost — you just can't make new AI requests until you have tokens.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
Is there a referral program?
Yes! Share your referral code and both you and your friend get 100,000 bonus tokens. You can refer up to 50 people for a total of 5,000,000 free tokens.
