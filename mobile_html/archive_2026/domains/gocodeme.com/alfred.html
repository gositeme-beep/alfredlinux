Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

Absolutely not. Alfred understands plain English (and French). Say "install WordPress" and it's done.

Can Alfred break my website?

Alfred confirms destructive actions, recommends backups, and you can always roll back with Git. Plus, our support team is one message away.

What are tokens and will I run out?

Tokens measure AI usage. Free plan includes 50,000 tokens. Builder plan: 300,000. Most users never hit their limit.

Can Alfred really generate images?

Yes. GoCodeMe's proprietary AI infrastructure creates photos, logos, banners, and product images from text. Saved directly to your site.

Is this better than Cursor or Lovable?

For web hosting, dramatically. Cursor can't manage hosting, domains, email, SSL, or generate images. We do all of that plus 150+ hosting tools, and hosting is included.

Is this like n8n or Make.com?

No — those are workflow tools. Alfred is a full AI hosting platform: editor + assistant + hosting + images + billing, all in one browser tab.

What if I need more tokens?

Upgrade to Professional (600K), Studio (1.5M), Business (3M), or Enterprise (5M). Custom plans available on request.

What model powers Alfred?

17 AI engines across 3 providers — GoCodeMe's proprietary multi-model AI infrastructure powers text, code, image, video, and audio generation. We route to the best model for each task automatically.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. GoCodeMe editor works in any modern browser — phone, iPad, laptop. Alfred from anywhere.

Is my data safe?

Hardened Canadian infrastructure. Sandboxed access. Encrypted connections. We don't sell data or train on your files.

Can I really control my website from WhatsApp?

Yes. And Signal, Discord, Telegram, and SMS too. Send a message like "check my traffic" or "install a plugin" and Alfred responds in seconds.

How do voice commands work?

Built into your GoCodeMe editor browser session. Click the microphone, speak naturally — "Set up SSL on mysite.com" — and Alfred executes it. No extensions. No plugins. Just your browser and your voice.

What is voice steering?

Voice steering lets you queue commands while Alfred is executing. Say 'stop' to cancel, or keep talking to queue your next task. Alfred processes them in order — no waiting.

Does Alfred support e-commerce?

Yes. Full WooCommerce, Stripe, shipping zones, tax configs, revenue dashboards — all by voice or text.
