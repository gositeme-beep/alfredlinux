## 🔗 Cross-Navigation Flow

### **Complete User Journey**
1. **Start at Judicial Directory** → Browse overview statistics
2. **Switch to Legal Professionals** → Explore individual lawyers
3. **Click on lawyer** → View detailed individual profile
4. **See business affiliations** → Click on business links
5. **View business profile** → See team members
6. **Click on team members** → Back to individual profiles
7. **Switch to Law Firms** → Browse business directory
8. **Click on firm** → View detailed business profile
9. **See team members** → Click on individual lawyers
10. **Complete the circle** → Seamless ecosystem navigation

### **Navigation Patterns**
- **Individual → Business → Individual** (Professional relationships)
- **Business → Individual → Business** (Team exploration)
- **Directory → Detail → Directory** (Discovery and research)
- **Overview → Specific → Overview** (Contextual exploration)
